Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a workout?

You can create workouts in two ways:

  • AI Workout: Tap "AI Workout" and describe what you want to train. Our AI will create a personalized workout for you.
  • Manual Workout: Tap "Manual Workout" to create a workout from scratch by adding exercises manually.

How does AI exercise detection work?

Take a photo of gym equipment or an exercise, and our AI will automatically detect what exercise you're doing. You can also select an exercise manually from the exercise library.

How do I track my progress?

Go to the "Progress" tab to see your weight progression charts, muscle focus analysis, and personal records over time.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Yes! Your workout data is automatically synced to the cloud when you're logged in, so you can access it from any device.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@gymvision.ai with your account email address.
